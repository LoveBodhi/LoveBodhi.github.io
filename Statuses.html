Status Explanation

1. Waiting: Waiting for announcing result.
2. Done: In TMESF, also known as Pipeline.
3. In VerYYYYmmdd: A premade tone is available in Version YYYYmmdd. Later might have a replacement.
4. WIP: Working in progress.
5. Not Started: Not ready to make.
6. Restricted: All samples are either restricted or only exists in commercial sources, which cannot be used. Only can be resynthesized.
7. Sampleless: Never heard outside 3rd party modules. Only can be resynthesized.
8. Stuck in loop / phrase: All individal non-Public Domain samples stucks in loop or phrases. Only can be resynthesized.
9. Finding Tones: Menu Patches specific. Needs to find individal tones.
10. Need Alt: A least of sample from 3rd party module, which needs replacement of non-3rd party module.
