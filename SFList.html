Free / Non-Free Soundfont List

Here is a list of soundfonts, both free and non-free.

Free:
SGM (Used widely)
TMESF (100% no Roland samples, GPL)
DSF (Ultimate and GE are GPL, others are PD)
Airfont (PD)
Unison (PD)
Xioad (PD)
JNS-GM 2 (PD)
KOR GM Soundfont (GPL)

Non-Free:
The Fairy Tale Bank 1&2 (CC-BY-NC-SA 4.0)
Arachno (Non-commerical use only since it samples from third material)
Crisis GM (Non-commerical use only + unmodifiable)
Titanic (Non-commerical use only)
Timbres of Heaven (Unmirrorable outside of official website)
GMR Basico (Too many limitation, e.g. unsharable, commerical unusable)
OmegaGMGS2 (Mix from various 3rd party materials)
Chorium (Includes Roland samples)
All game soundfonts are ripped, which are grey area

Unknown:
Quasisynth GM (Unknown)
HiDef (A mix of Roland, ToH, Quasisynth GM, etc, includes materials that can only be used in non-commerical purpose)
32MbGMStereo (Unknown)
GM Thai Mix (Unknown)
