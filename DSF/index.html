DSOUNDFONT Family

Welcome to DSOUNDFONT Family in Stardream Studio's Page!

DSOUNDFONT is a series that created by Strix SoundFont Team.

The DSOUNDFONT families can be downloaded in Musical Artifacts.

DSOUNDFONT Types
Type Version Description
Lite 2.51 Pure GM SoundFont. WTFPL (Public Domain).
Standard 4.04 GS SoundFont. Public Domain.
Plus 4.03 Same as Standard, the only addition is non-abriged samples. Public Domain.
Gaming
 3.51 Designed for game use. different samples such as C5 Lite. GS, PCM, MT-32 support. Mixed sources (Mainly is GPL V3+ with exception, some are MIT and CC-BY-SA. Both are free licences).
Ultimate
 Final
 All-in-one SoundFont set, SC-88Pro support, Yamaha-esqued tones and 8850 tones. Natural C5. Mixed sources (Mainly is GPL V3+ with exception, some are MIT and CC-BY-SA. Both are free licences).
Since the next project is renamed to "Enchant!" (A completely new project), 4.21 is the Final Version of DSoundFont Ultimate.

Notes:

DSOUNDFONT Gaming Edition doesn't contain OSDK Drum Kits.
DSOUNDFONT Ultimate have 3rd party module samples (Such as The Fairy Tale Bank 2).
