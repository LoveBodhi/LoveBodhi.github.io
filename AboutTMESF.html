About The Multi-Elemental Soundfont

The Multi-Elemental Soundfont (TMESF in short) is a set of all-in-one soundfonts. It's a split-bank.
Unlike DSoundFont Ultimate, All parts are below 2GB, which means it's compatible with most soundfont players.

It features the most natural, realistic, even a few of synthsized sounds. None of 3rd party modules are used, expect M1 Piano.

Suitable for: Electronic / Dance / EDM, Classical, Funk / Pop, Jazz, Rock, Metal, Fairy Tale Themed, Cinematic, 80/90's Retro, etc.

TMESF is not only Roland SC-8850 map patches. Instead, It's mixed with SC-8850 Map and other variations, like XG patches, PSR-S975 patches (Except Super Atriculation Patches), etc. Me and Strix's original patches are also included (and it's Shared with DSoundFont Ultimate).

As Strix said, For CM-64 / MT-32 Support, use DSoundFont Gaming Edition.
For Roland samples usage, use HiDef. For XG Support, buy a Yamaha Synthsizer or Yamaha Keyboard with XG / XGLite.

Notice: If a least of the sample is from 3rd party modules (Like Roland SC, Yamaha MU, etc.) exist, I'll replace or remove the corresponding sample as possible.
