Status Explanation

1. Waiting: Waiting for announcing result.
2. Done: In TMESF, also known as Pipeline.
3. In VerYYYYmmdd: A premade tone is available in Version YYYYmmdd. Later might have a replacement.
4. WIP: Working in progress.
5. Not Started: Not ready to make.
6. Restricted: All samples are either restricted or only exists in commercial sources, which cannot be used.
7. Sampleless: Either never heard outside 3rd party modules or no sample exist in freesound.
8. Sampleless (Number): Partially sampleless.
9. No suitable smp.: No suitable samples. Basically non-3rd party samples.
10. Stuck in loop / phrase: All individal non-Public Domain samples stucks in loop or phrases.
11. Finding Tones: Menu Patches specific. Needs to find individal tones.
12. Need Alternative: A least of sample from 3rd party module, which needs replacement.
13. Lack Vel. Range: No velocity range found. "Jazz Scat" exclusive.
14. Replanned: A next version will be replaced.

The only way is resynthesize them (DIY), if the instrument is in statuses 6-10.
